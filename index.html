Public Engagement Gap

Nearly 60% of draft regulations receive zero public comments, indicating significant barriers to civic participation in the consultation process.

Potential Causes

  • Limited public awareness
  • Technical language barriers
  • Short consultation periods
  • Perceived lack of impact

Recommendations

  • Proactive notification systems
  • Plain language summaries
  • Extended comment periods
  • Publish response to comments
